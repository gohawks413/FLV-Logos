This repository has the logos for First Light Valorant. This is mainly for the production crew.

FLV  is a Valorant community series catering to players of all ranks and experience levels. We use a Player Rating (PR) system to facilitate competitive and fair matches between teams of players with drastic skill gaps. Matches are played once a week and are scheduled by team Captains finding an optimal time for both teams. There are no prizes. A season consists of several weeks of regular season followed by playoffs to determine the season champion.
